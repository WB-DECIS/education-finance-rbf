The World’ Bank’s education taxonomy has been used as basis for coding Focus areas and topics with some adjustments to better reflect the focus of DLIs and DLRs (Disbursement Link Results).

Focus area Definition
Education Access and Equity Definition: Interventions that address supply and/or demand issues in educational access and equity.
Topics include: Inclusive Education; Education in Post Conflict Situations; Education in Emergency Situations; Indigenous Education; Out-of-School Children and Youth; Multi-Grade Education; Alternative Education; Education for Vulnerable and Disadvantaged Groups; Education and Gender; Rural Education
Education Financing Definition: Initiatives to improve the funding structures of education systems and educational institutions at all levels.
Topics include: Institutional Grants, Results-based/Performance-Based Financing, Education Funding Formulae, Public Finance Management, User Fees in Education, Student Loan Program
Student Assessment Definition: Activities that strengthen countries, teachers, and schools’ abilities to collect and use information on student learning through assessments. This includes examinations on the tertiary level and skills certification.
Topics include: International Student Assessment, National Student Assessment, Classroom Assessment and Examination, School Readiness, Skills Certification, Examinations
Skills Development Definition: Skills development is a life–long process that proceeds in stages, each building on the previous one. Foundational skills comprise the basic cognitive skills, such as literacy and numeracy, and socio-emotional skills; job-specific skills (hard skills) describe a person's technical skill set and ability to perform specific job-related tasks.
Topics include: Second Chance Education for Young Adults, On the Job Training, Reskilling, Dual Training, Apprenticeship, Qualification Frameworks, Employability, Soft Skills, Tracer Study
Teachers Definition: Activities to recruit, train, deploy, motivate, and evaluate teachers.
Topics include: Teacher Standards, Teacher Accreditation and Certification, Teacher Recruitment and Deployment, Teacher Salaries and Incentives, Teacher Performance Assessment, Pre-service Teacher Training, In-service Teacher Training, Training of Teacher Educators, Restructuring Teacher Training Systems, Training of Vocational Skills Instructors, Pedagogical Training for University Professors, Teacher Unions and Professional Associations
Standards, Curriculum and Textbooks Definition: Activities that relate to the content delivered in educational institutions including learning standards, curriculum, textbooks and other teaching/learning materials. This includes initiatives to improve the relevance of education content at all levels of the system.
Topics include: Learning Standards; Curriculum Development and Reforms, Teaching and Learning Materials; Language of Instruction; Pedagogical Materials; Textbooks; Digital Learning and E-content
Education Governance, School-Based Management Definition: Education systems need to ensure minimum levels of learning across institutions and sub-sectors. Operational standards for schools, training institutions and universities can be developed to clearly define minimum standards. Supervision, accountability and/or accreditation systems monitor the quality of institutions and provide feedback to students, parents, schools, administration, and policymakers.
Topics include: Education Reform; Education Strategic Planning and Management; Monitoring and Evaluation in Education, Operational standards for educational institutions , School/Institutional autonomy and accountability, School-based management and school development planning, School Directors, School supervision systems and/or inspection, Results-based management of schools and the education system, University quality assurance and accreditation systems, Education Management Information Systems (EMIS), Local Government, Education Districts, Community Outreach
Education Facilities Definition: The construction, rehabilitation, and equipment of educational institutions on all levels from early childhood education to tertiary.
Topics include: Classroom construction/rehabilitation, Furnishings (desks/chairs) and equipment of educational institutions, Water and sanitation systems, Libraries and resource centers, Housing and boarding facilities for teachers and students, School maintenance programs, Facilities for education administration
Science and Technology Definition: Activities to promote scientific and technological knowledge and innovation inside and outside of educational institutions. They can range from initiatives to improve science curriculum and instruction in secondary schools to research and development (R&D) grants at the tertiary level.
Topics include: Science, technology and innovation (STI) policy; Institutional development of STI institutions; STI grants; Research and development (R&D); capacity building partnerships between STI organizations; training STI knowledge workers; laboratories and science lab equipment
Private Sector Delivery of Education Definition: Interventions that relate to the provision of education by groups outside of the public sector such as NGOs, community organizations, faith-based organizations, and for-profit companies. This includes independent private schools, government-funded private schools, privately managed schools, and voucher schools.
Topics include: Public-private partnerships in education management; Public-private partnerships in education financing; private sector provision of education on all levels; Accountability and quality assurance for private educational institutions on all levels; Licensing and accreditation for private schools, universities, and skills training providers; Private sector delivery of skills training or apprenticeships; Vouchers for vocational training at private training institutions; Grants to private institutions for meeting performance and/or reporting targets; Private sector input into curriculum reforms to improve relevance of education to employment
Other Definition: Activities focus on project's implementation structure, and that do not fit under other focus area can be included under the Other Education.
Topics include: Establishment, staffing, and operation of Project Management Units for World Bank Projects; Monitoring and Evaluation of World Bank Project Activities; Communications with stakeholders involved in World Bank Projects.
Results chain position Definition
Action Action, process, or performance indicators typically measure decisions, performance, enactment of program guidelines, or the general adoption of key measures needed to ensure the functioning of a government program
Process Process indicators typically include measures to strengthen the performance of the government FM, procurements processes, environmental and social aspects, oversight and controls, or M&E
Output Outputs are the supply-side deliverables, including products, or services that result from a development intervention (e.g., construction of a school, deveopment of a framework, curriculum etc). Output indicators generally describe the number or type of outputs produced. Examples include number of teachers provided training, number of centers established, textbooks developed etc.
Intermediate Outcome Outcomes that describe the benefits expected in terms of change in condition or behavior of the program beneficiaries that result from the access to and use of outputs: e.g. increase in enrollment, increase in percentage of teacher using new techniques etc.
Outcome Outcome indicators which are defined at the PDO level and measure one level of a program’s outcomes achieved by the end of the program
