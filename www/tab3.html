Disbursement-linked Indicators (DLI) are "specific, measurable, and verifiable indicators.... expressed as outcomes, outputs, intermediate outcomes or outputs, process indicators, or financing indicators. DLIs may also be defined as actions or process results deemed critical for strengthening performance under an RBF modality (this could include actions for improving fiduciary, social and environmental issues and/or monitoring and evaluation), or as indicators of key institutional changes."1
